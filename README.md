## Motivation

Creating an AWS account is the first step towards leveraging the power of cloud computing. AWS offers a wide range of services that allow you to build, deploy, and manage applications at scale. Whether you're a developer, a startup, or an enterprise, AWS provides the tools and resources needed to innovate and grow your business.

## Benefits of AWS

- **Scalability:** Easily scale your applications as your business grows.
- **Security:** Built-in security features help you protect your data and applications.
- **Cost-Effective:** Pay only for what you use, with flexible pricing options.
- **Global Reach:** Deploy your applications across the globe with AWS's extensive network.

## Getting Started

To begin, follow the [Creating an AWS Account](./docs/aws-account-setup.md) guide. This will walk you through the steps needed to set up your AWS account securely.
